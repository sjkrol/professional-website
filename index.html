Research Fellow in the Department of Human-Centered Computing at Monash University, specializing in machine learning and human-computer interaction.

My research focuses on developing machine learning solutions to assist creatives in their practices. I take a human-centered approach to AI, ensuring that the systems I develop are not only technically sound but also solve real problems for working practitioners.

Prior to this position, I completed my PhD in Artificial Intelligence at Monash University under the supervision of Prof. Jon McCormack and A/Prof. Teresa Llano.
